# Service Agreement

## Article 1: Definitions

"Service Provider" refers to TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation.
"Client" refers to the party purchasing services under this agreement.
"Services" means the software development and consulting services described in Exhibit A.

## Article 2: Scope of Services

The Service Provider agrees to provide the following services:

- Custom software development as specified in the project requirements
- Technical consulting for system architecture
- Monthly maintenance and support
- Training sessions for client staff

## Article 3: Payment Terms

Client agrees to pay Service Provider as follows:

- Initial deposit: 30% upon signing
- Progress payment: 40% upon milestone completion
- Final payment: 30% upon project completion
- Late payments subject to 1.5% monthly interest

## Article 4: Intellectual Property

All intellectual property created during the engagement shall be owned by the Client upon full payment. Service Provider retains the right to use general knowledge and skills gained during the project.

## Article 5: Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during the engagement. This obligation survives termination of the agreement for a period of 3 years.

## Article 6: Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the termination date.
